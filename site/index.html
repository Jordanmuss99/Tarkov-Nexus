2

Connect

Open tarkov.dev, go to any map, and paste your Remote ID into the app

3

Play

Load into a raid and take a screenshot (default: PrintScreen)

4

See Your Position

Your marker instantly appears on the tarkov.dev map at your exact coordinates

How does it work?

When you take a screenshot in Tarkov, the game saves your coordinates in the filename. Tarkov Nexus watches for new screenshots and sends those coordinates to tarkov.dev in real-time.
